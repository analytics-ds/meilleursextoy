## How to choose the right strap-on?

Before comparing brands, ask the right questions. The type of system and the material matter far more than the name on the box.

### Harness, kit or strapless?

The strap **harness** gives the firmest hold: ideal for wide, confident movements. The harness-plus-dildo **kit** is the turnkey solution to start. The **strapless** frees both partners from any strap, at the cost of a muscular hold to master. Our advice: a harness to begin, a strapless when you want more closeness.

### Material and care

Aim for **medical silicone**, non-porous and boilable. It keeps no bacteria or odours, unlike PVC. To care for your gear without damaging it, follow our tips on [how to clean a sex toy](/en/blog/nettoyer-sextoy/). A fabric harness often goes in the machine, which makes life simpler.

### Dildo size and compatibility

For a first model, a length of 10 to 15 cm and a diameter of 3 to 4 cm are plenty. Check the diameter the harness ring accepts, or choose a system with **interchangeable rings** so your dildo can evolve without buying everything again.
