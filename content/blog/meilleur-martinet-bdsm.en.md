## How to choose your BDSM flogger

Three things make the difference between a good flogger and a pointless duster.

The **leather and the weight of the falls** first. Soft leather with wide tails gives a thuddy impact, almost a firm massage. Thin, weighted falls bite and sting. Faux leather lets you explore without sharp pain. Many end up with two floggers, one soft and one severe, to vary the sensations.

The **balance of the handle** next. A good handle sits without straining the wrist. Turned wood, sheathed metal or braided leather, the weight must counterbalance the falls so the stroke stays precise.

**Safety** last. The flogger targets fleshy areas (buttocks, upper thighs, back), never the kidneys or the neck. Progressive warm-up, light strokes first, an agreed safety word.
