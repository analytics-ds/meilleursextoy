## How we selected these sex toys

We didn't pick at random from a catalog. Every model was ordered, unboxed and used across several sessions. Here are our concrete criteria.

- **Material and safety:** body-safe silicone only, phthalate-free and hypoallergenic. Nothing else goes inside your body.
- **Power and precision:** we rated real stimulation, not marketing promises.
- **Battery life and charge:** a USB-rechargeable battery that goes the distance, no impossible-to-find proprietary cable.
- **Maintenance:** a waterproof device that's easy to clean with lukewarm water counts enormously.
- **Discretion:** noise level, storage format and discreet delivery from the brand.

Storage and cleaning matter as much as the pleasure of the moment. A poorly maintained toy is a toy you stop using. We also checked that every product was compatible with a water-based lubricant, the only kind that doesn't attack silicone.

## FAQ

### What's the best sex toy for a beginner?

The Satisfyer Penguin at 35 €. Simple, powerful for its price, discreet format and easy to clean. You discover air pulse without breaking the bank or overcomplicating things. If you click with it, you'll move up the range later.

### Air pulse or classic vibrator, which to choose?

It depends on your sensitivity. Air pulse stimulates the clitoris without direct contact, ideal if you're sensitive. The classic vibrator offers wider, deeper vibrations, perfect for spread-out stimulation or the G-spot. Many people end up owning both.

### Which lubricant to use with a silicone sex toy?

Always a water-based lubricant. It's compatible with everything, doesn't damage silicone and stays easy to clean. Avoid silicone and oil, which degrade the material of your toy over time.

### How to clean and maintain your sex toy?

Lukewarm water and mild soap after every use, especially if your model is waterproof. Dry it well, store it in a clean pouch. Good maintenance extends the lifespan and keeps the toy hypoallergenic and clean. Make sure it's charged before storing it for a long time.

### Is delivery really discreet?

Yes. All serious brands ship in neutral packaging, no logo or mention of the contents. Discreet delivery is the industry standard. Nobody will know what's inside the box.
