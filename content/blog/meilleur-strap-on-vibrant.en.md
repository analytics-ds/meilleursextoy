## How to choose a vibrating strap-on?

### Dual stimulation or receiver only?

This is the first decision. If you want both partners to feel the vibrations, you need a motorised strapless with an internal bulb, like the Strap-on-me Multi Orgasm or the Fun Factory Share Vibe Plus. If the priority is the receiver, a vibrating dildo on a harness is enough and costs less.

### Strapless or harness?

The strapless is more connected but takes some practice for the hold. The harness is more stable and reassuring to start. Many begin with a harness kit before moving to a strapless. Our guide to [choosing your sex toy](/en/blog/choisir-sextoy/) sets out the useful basics for your body and desires.

### Material and care

Always aim for phthalate-free medical silicone: non-porous, it cleans easily and does not hold bacteria. Avoid dildos in unidentified porous material. Our tips to [clean a sex toy](/en/blog/nettoyer-sextoy/) also apply to vibrating models, avoiding immersing the motor if the product is not waterproof.
